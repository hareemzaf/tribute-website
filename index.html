Sir Zia Khan

the amazing person

Sir Zia Khan is the Senior Vice President for Innovation at The Rockefeller Foundationwith 13 years of experience in strategy and program leadership. He focuses on leveraging novel technologies for transformative impact in areas such as clean energy access, food waste reduction, and inclusive economic opportunities. Zia serves on the boards of organizations like Atlas AI and DataKind, and is an advisory board member for institutions like University College London Institute for Innovation and Public Purpose. He previously led the San Francisco office at Katzenbach Partners and co-authored the book "Leading Outside the Lines." Zia holds a B.S. from Cornell University and an M.S. and Ph.D. from Stanford University. His contributions to innovation and social impact span various domains, making him a key figure in the field.
